Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common questions.

A short overview of our service and operations. For any additional detail, our team is available 24/7 via email or WhatsApp.

Do you offer fixed prices for Catania Airport transfers? + −

Yes. All our Catania Airport transfers are quoted in advance with fixed prices, including taxes, tolls and standard waiting time. There are no hidden extras added after the ride.

How do you handle delayed or early flights? + −

We monitor flight status in real time. Your chauffeur adjusts the pick‑up time accordingly and waits inside the arrivals hall within the agreed waiting window, so your guests are always met even when the schedule changes.

Can you support groups and events of different sizes? + −

Yes. We coordinate sedans, vans and buses through our partner network, from small executive meetings to large‑scale events. A dedicated coordinator can oversee complex schedules when needed.

Do you provide child seats if required? + −

Child and infant seats can be provided on request at the time of booking. Let us know the age and number of children so we can assign the correct equipment and vehicle type.

Are you available 24/7? + −

Our operations team monitors transfers around the clock and we can organise services at any time of day or night, subject to advance booking and availability.
